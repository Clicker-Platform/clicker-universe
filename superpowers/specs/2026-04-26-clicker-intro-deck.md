# Clicker Introduction Deck — Spec

**Date:** 2026-04-26
**Type:** Presentation / Pitch Deck
**Audience:** SME owners (F&B, salons, workshops, retail, clinics) — non-technical
**Tone:** Empathetic + aspirational — warm, human, but confident
**Length:** 12 slides · ~8 minutes to present
**Structure:** A+B hybrid — Problem → Hero (via character story)

---

## Narrative Spine

> Open with the pain. Make them feel seen. Then walk them through one business owner's day — where every problem gets solved by Clicker. Close by showing the full platform and inviting them in.

Four acts:

| Act | Slides | Theme |
|-----|--------|-------|
| 1 — The World They Live In | 1–3 | Problem: fragmentation + growth gap |
| 2 — Meet Moli | 4–8 | Story: one day, five Clicker moments |
| 3 — What Makes This Possible | 9–11 | Platform: modules, verticals, differentiators |
| 4 — Start Today | 12 | CTA: echo tagline, next step |

---

## Protagonist

**Moli** — a woman who runs a small-to-medium business (café/F&B context works as the anchor, but the story is written so any SME owner can see themselves in her). She has great products, loyal customers, and dreams of growth — but is drowning in admin and disconnected tools.

---

## Slide-by-Slide Outline

### Act 1 — The World They Live In

**Slide 1 — Cover**
- Headline: *"Run your business, not your apps."*
- Clicker logo + tagline, clean and bold
- Sets aspirational tone immediately

**Slide 2 — The Problem**
- Headline: *"Sound familiar?"*
- Visual: scattered app icons, WhatsApp orders, manual spreadsheets, no real website
- Message: 5+ different apps, no unified view, chaotic day-to-day
- Audience sees themselves here — this slide should feel like recognition, not a lecture

**Slide 3 — The Cost**
- Headline: *"Fragmentation is expensive."*
- Three hidden costs named: time lost switching tools, customers lost to friction, subscriptions adding up with no single business view
- Tone: empathetic — naming the pain, not shaming the owner

---

### Act 2 — Meet Moli

**Slide 4 — Character Intro**
- Headline: *"Meet Moli."*
- She runs a café. Great coffee, loyal regulars, a dream of opening a second branch.
- But: she's juggling apps, missing bookings, losing track of who her best customers are.
- This is the bridge from problem to solution.

**Slide 5 — Story Beat 1: Website + Booking**
- Headline: *"A customer books a table — from Moli's own website."*
- Clicker gave Moli a professional website with a live booking widget. No developer, no waiting.
- Clicker features highlighted: **Canvas Studio** (page builder) + **Reservation module**

**Slide 6 — Story Beat 2: POS + Kitchen**
- Headline: *"Lunch rush hits — orders flow straight to the kitchen."*
- Customers scan a QR code, order from their phone. The kitchen display shows live orders. No shouting, no paper slips.
- Clicker features highlighted: **BYOD POS** + **Kitchen Display System (KDS)**

**Slide 7 — Story Beat 3: Loyalty**
- Headline: *"A regular earns loyalty points — automatically."*
- Every purchase adds points. Customers come back for rewards. Moli didn't configure anything complex — it just works.
- Clicker features highlighted: **Membership & Loyalty module**

**Slide 8 — Story Beat 4: AI Agent**
- Headline: *"An AI agent answers customer questions — while Moli sleeps."*
- The AI Sales Agent handles FAQs, captures leads, qualifies prospects 24/7. Moli wakes up to a list of interested customers.
- Clicker features highlighted: **AI Sales Agent module**

---

### Act 3 — What Makes This Possible

**Slide 9 — Platform Overview**
- Headline: *"One platform. Every tool your business needs."*
- Module grid showing all available capabilities:
  - Website Builder (Canvas Studio)
  - Point of Sale (BYOD POS + KDS)
  - Reservations & Booking
  - Membership & Loyalty
  - Inventory Management
  - Sales Pipeline (CRM)
  - Service Records (automotive/service vertical)
  - AI Sales Agent
  - AI Marketing
- Message: you pick the modules you need. Start small, grow into more.

**Slide 10 — Works for Any Business**
- Headline: *"F&B. Salon. Workshop. Retail. Clinic."*
- 4–5 business types shown with the relevant module combination for each:
  - **Café / F&B** → POS + Loyalty + AI Agent + Website
  - **Salon / Spa** → Reservation + Membership + Website
  - **Auto Workshop** → Service Records + Inventory + CRM
  - **Retail** → POS + Inventory + Loyalty + Website
  - **Clinic / Service** → Reservation + CRM + AI Agent
- Reinforces versatility without feeling generic

**Slide 11 — Why Clicker**
- Headline: *"Built for how you actually run your business."*
- Three differentiators:
  1. **All-in-one** — no more juggling apps, one place for everything
  2. **Grows with you** — enable modules as your business needs them
  3. **Your brand** — your website, your identity, powered by Clicker

---

### Act 4 — Start Today

**Slide 12 — CTA / Closing**
- Headline: *"Ready to run your business, not your apps?"*
- Echo of the opening tagline — creates a satisfying close
- Callback to Moli: *"Moli did it. So can you."*
- Clear next step: website URL, QR code to sign up, or contact info

---

## Design Direction

- **Tone:** Warm but premium — not startup-cold, not corporate-stiff
- **Character:** Moli should feel real and relatable, not a stock photo cliché
- **Slide 2 visual:** Scattered app icons / sticky notes — maximally chaotic to contrast with the clean Clicker slides that follow
- **Story slides (5–8):** Each should feel like a mini-moment — a snapshot of Moli's day, with the Clicker feature as the quiet hero
- **Module grid (slide 9):** Clean icon grid — show breadth without overwhelming

---

## Out of Scope

- Pricing details (unless added as a separate appendix slide)
- Technical architecture
- Competitor comparison
- Detailed feature documentation
